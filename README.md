# Legal Prompt Library

A curated collection of practical AI prompts for attorneys — organized by task, copy-paste ready, and sourced from publicly shared attorney experiences.

## How to Use

1. Find the category that matches your task
2. Copy the prompt template
3. Replace `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDERS]` with your specifics
4. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred LLM
5. Always review and verify AI output before relying on it professionally

## ⚠️ Important Disclaimers

- **Verify everything.** AI can hallucinate case citations, statutes, and legal standards. Always independently verify legal authorities before relying on them.
- **Confidentiality.** Do not paste client confidential information into public AI tools. Use enterprise/private deployments with appropriate data protection agreements.
- **Jurisdiction matters.** Many prompts reference specific jurisdictions. Adapt them to your applicable law.
- **Not a substitute for judgment.** These prompts are starting points. Attorney review, supervision, and professional judgment are always required.
