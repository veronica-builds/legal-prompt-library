# Corporate & Transactional Prompts

Prompts for M&A, corporate governance, employment law, and business transactions.

---

## M&A Due Diligence Checklist

```
List key legal due diligence questions and document requests for a potential acquisition
of a [TARGET COMPANY TYPE — e.g., SaaS company / manufacturing business / healthcare startup].

Organize by category:
1. Corporate organization and capitalization
2. Intellectual property ownership and licensing
3. Material contracts (customers, vendors, partners)
4. Litigation and regulatory proceedings
5. Employment and labor matters
6. Real estate and assets
7. Tax compliance
8. Data privacy and cybersecurity
9. Regulatory licenses and permits
10. Financial statements and liabilities

For each category, list the top 5–10 questions and documents to request.
```

*Source: Ten Things Blog*

## Earnout Clause Analysis

```
Explain what an earnout is in the context of M&A and what legal protections a seller
should negotiate in the earnout provisions.

Context:
- Transaction type: [DESCRIBE]
- Proposed earnout: [DESCRIBE METRICS AND TIMELINE]
- Buyer's business: [DESCRIBE]

Address:
1. Definition and measurement of earnout metrics
2. Buyer's covenant to operate the business to achieve the earnout
3. Accounting methodology and dispute resolution
4. Acceleration events (change of control, breach)
5. Anti-dilution and anti-sandbagging protections
```
